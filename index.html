Fort Collins Environmental Collective

Science-grounded, inclusive environmental leadership for our city.

FoCo ECo advances regenerative, nature-positive solutions for Fort Collins—combining sound science, systems thinking, and community equity to shape policy, restore habitat, and train the next generation of advocates.

About Us

Designing a resilient, nature-positive Fort Collins

We are a collective of neighbors, scientists, planners, and organizers focused on building a regenerative city. Our mission is to pair evidence-based advocacy with deep community collaboration—supporting climate-smart land use, healthy riverscapes, equitable access to nature, and progressive local policy.

Our vision: a living system where city form, ecosystems, and social equity are designed together—empowering residents to co-create an abundant, inclusive, and resilient future for Northern Colorado.

  • Evidence-based decisions
  • Environmental equity & inclusivity
  • Collaboration & coalition building
  • Outcomes-oriented action
Strategic Focus

Where we focus our energy

Our projects blend policy advocacy, public education, and on-the-ground activation to protect habitat, restore waters, and expand equitable access to nature.

Nature in the City

Advance habitat-rich urban design—native plant cover, pollinator corridors, and greenspace in every neighborhood.

Climate-Smart Land Use

Champion walkable, transit-oriented growth that reduces emissions, water use, and sprawl while improving livability.

Riverscape Health

Restore the Poudre—boost baseflows, partner on watershed-scale projects, and safeguard water quality.

Environmental Equity

Expand urban forestry, clean air programs, and hazard mitigation to ensure all residents benefit from a healthy city.

Civic Engagement

Train emerging advocates to navigate civic processes, join boards, and lead on local environmental decisions.

Coalition Building

Partner with progressive organizations to educate, organize, and mobilize around key environmental policies.
